

1. Inheritance：it defines a relationship between objects that share characteristics. 
2. Superclass：an existing class
3. Subclass：a new class, called a subclass, is created from an existing class, called a superclass 
4. extends : The process of extending from superclass to subclass
5. super()：A subclass can call a method in its superclass by using super
